Weather Trends

Temperature, Precipitation, and Drought

This collection of tools builds on monthly values of temperature, precipitation, and several drought indices for each of the 344 climate divisions in the contiguous United States. These can be aggregated into statewide, climate region, and contiguous U.S. values.

Temperature, Precipitation, and Drought Time Series: View how a certain parameter in a certain place has changed since 1895. The user can define the month or season and place of interest..

Temperature and Precipitation Rankings: Ranks the warmest, coldest, driest, and wettest periods on record for each of the climate divisions, states, climate regions, and the contiguous United States. Place any month, season, or year into historical perspective.
